Almost a year later, we discovered that the UNAM Transparency Committee had indeed issued a response. The Resolution CTR / 49/2016 dated 29 August 2016 \cite{transparencia2016} stated that, despite acknowledging and confirming the 'public nature' of the information requested, the amount payable to Elsevier is considered to be 'reserved' information and therefore cannot be made public for a period of 5 years from the date of the resolution or until the contract ceases to be in force \cite{Priego2017}.
