As members of international academic communities of and of the general public at large, the undersigned, wish to document publicly our dissatisfaction with the lack of transparency with which the agreement between transnational corporation Elsevier and the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM)  was established on February 1, 2015. The agreement, under the UNGA
-Elsevier contract DGAJ-DPI-39-081114-241, saw the transfer of  "Production and accommodation, advertising and support "of 44 academic journals of open access of the UNAM (see the list here) to publishing company Elsevier.
