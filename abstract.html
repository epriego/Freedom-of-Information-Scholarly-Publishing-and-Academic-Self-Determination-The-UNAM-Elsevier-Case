As members of international academic communities specialising in scholarly communications, the authors wish to document publicly the agreement between transnational corporation Elsevier and the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM) established on February 1, 2015, and, according to sources that wish. The agreement, under the UNGA-Elsevier contract DGAJ-DPI-39-081114-241, saw the transfer of  "Production and accommodation, advertising and support "of 44 academic journals of open access of the UNAM (see the list here) to publishing company Elsevier.
